About Legal Linker

Legal Linker is a platform designed to connect individuals with the right legal professionals based on their needs. Users can search for advocates by location and case type, view profiles, and communicate directly with them. Legal Linker simplifies finding legal help, ensuring users have access to verified advocates and reliable information, making the legal process more accessible and transparent.
